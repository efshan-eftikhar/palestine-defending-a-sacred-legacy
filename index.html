Expose oppression, share real stories from Palestine, and unite the Ummah for justice — rooted in Islamic principles.

1. A Sacred Land

Palestine is the blessed land of the Prophets, home to Masjid al-Aqsa — Islam's first Qiblah and third holiest mosque. It's not just political — it's spiritual.

2. Quranic & Prophetic Importance

The Qur’an calls it the “Blessed Land.” Prophets including Ibrahim, Musa, Dawud, Sulayman, and Isa (peace be upon them) lived and preached here. Masjid al-Aqsa was the site of the Prophet ﷺ's night journey and imamat of all prophets.

3. Tabut-e-Sakina — The Lost Ark

The Ark of the Covenant was a miraculous chest from Allah containing sacred relics. It cannot be recreated — miracles are divine, not man-made.

4. Throne of Sulayman

Given by Allah, the throne was a symbol of knowledge and divine power. It cannot be rebuilt with today’s tools — it was a miracle, not technology.

5. Imamat at Al-Aqsa

During Isra wal Mi’raj, Prophet Muhammad ﷺ led all prophets in prayer at Al-Aqsa — a clear sign of unity and spiritual leadership centered in Palestine.

6. Final Thought

Palestine is not just history. It’s our identity. Defending it is defending Islam’s sacred legacy.
